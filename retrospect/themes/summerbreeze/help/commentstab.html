Comments Tab

The Comments Tab will open a page that will allow you to enter comments specific to the person of focus. Your email address is required and will be available publicly. You can add links to your comment by enclosing them within square brackets. Examples:
[http://www.google.com] = www.google.com
[http://www.google.com|Google] = Google

When you are finished, you may click the Submit button. In an effort to control spam, once you have finished the comment is submitted to the site owner for approval, prior to posting. The time it takes for review is based on the website owner not Retrospect-GDS
