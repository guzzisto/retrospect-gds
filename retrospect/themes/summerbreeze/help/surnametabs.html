Surname Tabs
 Previous  Next
The Surname List page displays a listing of the surnames found in the family history. When you first open the Surname List page you start at the Top 100 Tab surnames in the database. There are also tabs for each letter in the alphabet as well as an All Tab . Clicking on any of these tabs will take you to that page. In the body of the Surname List, you will find the surname listing for that tab and a number after each surname. This number is a count of the number of persons sharing that surname in the family history. Clicking on a surname in the body of th tab will take you to a listing of the persons in the family history that share that surname.
