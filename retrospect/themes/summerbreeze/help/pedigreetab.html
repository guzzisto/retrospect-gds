Pedigree Tab
 Previous  Next
Selecting the Pedigree Tab found in the Family Tab will open a page up displaying a Pedigree with the person of focus in the first or left most position. To the right will be the focus persons parents etc. Clicking on the name of a person in the pedigree will move that person to the number one position, making that person the focus person and allowing you to return to the Family Tab and review the information given on that person.

Moving to the Right
If there is a small circle attached to the rightmost pedigree position, this indicates there are additional names beyond the pedigree. Clicking on this circle will bring the next rightmost person onto the pedigree as the person of focus.

Moving to the Left
Moving to the left is only slightly more complicated. Return to the Family Tab and if there are children listed click on the child's name whom you want to make the focus person. Then click on the Pedigree Tab and that child will now be in the left most pedigree position.
