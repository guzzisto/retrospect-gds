The Family Tab displays more detailed information about the person selected in the Surname Tab. The person of focus is given above the tabs, across the top.

Person of Focus
Below the tabs is information specific to the focus person, including their birth, death and burial information. If available there will also be notes related to the focus person. If their parents are listed you may click on them for additional information specific to them. There may be bold numbers following this information. These numbers are the Source Citation identifiers which are detailed in the Source Citations section at the bottom of the page.

Family Section
Beneath the personal information of the focus person there may be information on their spouse and children. Names in bold may be clicked to make that person the focus person and take you to a page with their information on it.

Source Citations
The Source Citations section of the family page is a numbered listing of the source(s) cited in the above sections with regards to the focus person.
