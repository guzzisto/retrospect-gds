Top 100 Tab
 Previous  Next
Clicking on the Top 100 tab will take you to a listing of the top 100 surnames in the family history. In the body of the tab, you will find the surname listing for that tab and a number after each surname. This number is a count of the number of persons sharing that surname in the family history. Clicking on a surname in the tab will take you to a listing of the persons in the family history that share that surname.
