Reports Tab
 Previous  Next
Selecting the Reports Tab once you have selected a person of focus from the surname list allows you to select one of two types of reports. An Ancestor (Ahnentafel) Report and a Descendant Report.

Ancestor Reports
The Ancestor Report generates a listing of direct line ancestors beginning with the person of focus as Generation 1. Generation 2 would be the parents of the person of focus, with Generation 3 being the grand-parents, and so on. Enter the number of generations you want the report to list in the box labeled Generations:. The default value for this box is 250. Then click the Apply button to generate the report.

Within the Ancestor Report, names that are hi-lighted may be clicked to be taken to that persons Family Tab and make that person the person of focus.

Once the report has been generated, you can clear it by clicking on the Reports Tab again.

Descendant Reports
The Descendant Report generates a listing of direct line Descendants beginning with the person of focus as Generation 1. Generation 2 would be the children of the person of focus, with Generation 3 being the grand-children, and so on. Enter the number of generations you want the report to list in the box labeled Generations:. The default value for this box is 250. Then click the Apply button to generate the report.

Within the Descendant Report, names that are hi-lighted may be clicked to be taken to that persons Family Tab and make that person the person of focus.

Once the report has been generated, you can clear it by clicking on the Reports Tab again.
