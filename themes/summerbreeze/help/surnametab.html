Surname Tab
 Previous  Next
Selecting a surname from anyof the  Surname tabs takes you to a listing of all of the persons in the family history that share the surname you have clicked on. Each line of the list contains basic details of the information known about that person. This may include the surname, the first name, a symbol identifying whether the person is male or female, the date of birth, and the date of death.

To find more detailed information about a specific person, click on their name to go to that persons Family Tab. When in the Family Tab, Pedigree Tab, Reports Tab or Comments Tab you may return to the Surname list by clicking on the Surname Tab.
