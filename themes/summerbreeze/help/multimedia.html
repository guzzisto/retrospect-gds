Multimedia Tab
 Previous  Next
The Multimedia Tab is present after selecting a person of focus from the surname listing, when the Coppermine Photogallery Plug-in has been activated, and there is a photograph within the Photogallery that has been linked to the focus person. Clicking on the Multimedia tab will open up a thumbnail or thumbnails of the photo(s) linked to the person of focus. The number of photos linked is displayed in parethesis on the Multimedia Tab next to the tabs title.

Note: If the Coppermine Photogallery Plug-in has not been activated or if there is no picture associated with the person of focus, the Multimedia Tab will NOT be present.
