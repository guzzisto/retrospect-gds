At the top of each Retrospect-GDS page is a Top Menu consisting of Home, Search, Surname Tabs, Statistics, and Help. These options are detailed below

Home
If there is an introductory page, clicking on this link will take you to this introductory page. If there is no introductory page then clicking this link will take you to the Top 100 Tab of the Surname Tabs page.

Search
Clicking on the Search Button will take you to the Search page.

Surname List
Clicking on the Surname list button will take you to the Top 100 Tab of the Surname Tabs page.

Statistics
Clicking on the Statistics Button will take you to the Statistics page.

Help
Clicking on the Help Button will open this Help File.
